## Introduction

Mobile gaming has become increasingly popular, and fighting games are among the most exciting genres for gamers. This repository aims to highlight the best fighting games available for Android devices, providing enthusiasts with a handpicked selection of immersive and thrilling experiences.

## Game List

Here are some of the best fighting games for Android that you can explore:

1. [Shadow Fight 3](#)
   - Description: An action-packed game with stunning graphics and dynamic combat mechanics, allowing players to master various fighting styles.
2. [Tekken Mobile](#)
   - Description: Experience the legendary "Tekken" series on your Android device, featuring intense hand-to-hand combat and a diverse roster of characters.
3. [Injustice 2](#)
   - Description: Engage in epic battles between DC superheroes and villains, with impressive graphics, engaging storylines, and customizable heroes.
4. [Real Boxing 2 ROCKY](#)
   - Description: Step into the boxing ring with this game based on the iconic "Rocky" movie franchise, offering realistic boxing gameplay and immersive career mode.
5. [Mortal Kombat Mobile](#)
   - Description: The brutal and iconic "Mortal Kombat" series comes to Android, delivering intense fights, unique characters, and jaw-dropping fatalities.

Feel free to explore these games and share your experiences!
